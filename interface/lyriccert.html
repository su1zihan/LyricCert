How it works

Three steps. No setup.

From raw lyrics to a finished rating in one click.

01 · Paste

Drop in any lyrics.

Copy the words from any song, whether the full track or a single verse, and paste them into the analyzer. No accounts, no audio uploads.

02 · Analyze

The model reads through.

A long-context language model scores the song across four content dimensions: sexual, violence, substance, and language.

03 · Rate

Get a clear verdict.

The four scores collapse into one of five audience tiers, plus content warnings for any categories that scored high.

The rating system

Five tiers, plainly stated.

Clear audience tiers and content descriptors, adapted from the rating systems used for film and games.
